About the game

format_quote If I had to describe Mira, I would tell you about an extraordinary world that extends from the Sidereal Continent up to the north to the peaks of Mount Atlas to the south, passing through the great forest of Helios and the arid desert of Kera.",

In this fantasy adventure you will meet Tristan, a Protector of Sidra, called to complete a mission that will take him further than usual.

A classic RPG where you will experience unique places, solve puzzles, fight turn-based battles and live an exciting adventure.

Game features

  • Over 10 hours of gameplay
  • 30 unique places to explore
  • Dozens of puzzles to solve
  • Over 60 different enemies to face
  • Key items, consumables and equipment
  • Characters with specific classes and skills
  • Made by a solo developer
