SoundSpace questionnaire

This questionnaire is designed to help you understand the purpose of SoundSpace group and clearify your motivations for joining it.

Please read the manifest (reading takes about 2 minutes) to learn more about the group's rules and identity.

If your answers will not be aligned with the group's identity and rules, you will not be able to join it.

Prerequisites

  1. Download the Telegram app on your phone, if you haven't already.
  2. Register to create a new account.
  3. Note that the app is partially accessible; if you have problems with VoiceOver on iOS, activate the "Screen Recognition" feature in the rotor.

Guide to chat via desktop device (optional)

  1. Download Telegram for Windows(Unigram), if you don't have it already.
  2. Open the installed app.
  3. A QR code will be displayed on the screen.
  4. Open the Telegram app on your phone and go to Settings > Device > Add a device.
  5. Scan the QR code with the phone camera framing the desktop device.
  6. Once scanned, you can check the desktop device showing the chat interface.

After the device setup

  1. Proceed to complete the questionnaire on the next section using your phone or your desktop device.
  2. If you completed it successfully, press the appeared button to open Telegram.
  3. Request the access to the group.
  4. The administrator will grant the access in few hours
