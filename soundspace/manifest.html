Identity and Purpose

The group was created to build a healthy , welcoming, and stimulating community where it is enjoyable to write and read each other's messages.

The topics are audio games, accessibility, technology, and, more generally, the interests of visually impaired people.

Rules of Conduct

  1. It is mandatory to always maintain a kind, polite, and respectful attitude toward everyone.
  2. Summary or destructive criticism is not allowed; every opinion must be reasoned and open to discussion.
  3. Messages driven by excessive emotion, irrationality, or impulsiveness are not permitted.
  4. Allusive or provocative messages are prohibited.
  5. Sensationalist or extremist messages are forbidden.
  6. Statements presented as absolute truths, without leaving room for doubt or dialogue, are not allowed.
  7. Persistent or harassing behavior toward other members is prohibited.
  8. Respect for the fundamental rules of civil coexistence is implied: inappropriate, violent, offensive, or discriminatory content is prohibited.

Anyone who breaks or challenges these rules will be removed from the group without hesitation.

The rules may be updated over time, taking advantage of the experiences and exchanges among users.
